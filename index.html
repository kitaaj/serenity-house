About Serenity House

Serenity House is a personal wellness companion app designed to help you track your mood, journal your thoughts, and access supportive Reflective AI conversations. We're committed to creating a safe, private space for your wellness journey.

Our Commitment to You

We prioritize your privacy and security. Your data is stored locally on your device with optional cloud sync, and we never sell your personal information to third parties.

Key Features

  • Mood Tracking - Record and visualize your emotional patterns over time
  • Journaling - Write freely and receive supportive AI feedback
  • Reflective AI - Get AI-powered journaling prompts and self-reflection support
  • Offline-First - Access your data anytime, even without internet
  • Emergency Resources - Quick access to crisis helplines for your region

Important Disclaimer

Serenity House is not a substitute for professional healthcare. If you're experiencing a crisis, please contact emergency services or a licensed healthcare professional.
